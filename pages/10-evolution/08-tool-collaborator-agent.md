# Evolution of AI's role in the SDLC

```mermaid {scale: 0.7}
flowchart LR
  A["AI as a tool<br/>(autocomplete, Q&A)"] --> B["AI as a collaborator<br/>(IDE chat, refactor)"]
  B --> C["AI as an agent<br/>(multi-step tasks,<br/>tools, files, terminal)"]
```
